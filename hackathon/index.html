the CC-SHKR is a collaboration project made at the Copenhackathon, 16-17th og January, 2016. The idea for the service is was to recreate the random events that happened on swedish public television, where politician’s had children’s movie subtitles added by a mistake. We found the project fun to do, since the screenshots from TV already had us laughing. The people behind the project are: Daniel Nielsen, Kevin Hall and Magnus Nielsen. Sources: The Ministry of Denmark Open-Subtitles.org Subsmax.com
